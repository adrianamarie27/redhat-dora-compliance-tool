DORA Requirements:
Pillar 1: ICT Risk Management
Requirement: Documented and regularly reviewed ICT Risk Management Framework (RMF).
Requirement: Identification of all critical ICT assets, functions, and dependencies.
Requirement: Policy must define roles and responsibilities for the management body's oversight.
Pillar 2: ICT Incident Management
Requirement: Defined criteria for classifying 'major' ICT incidents.
Requirement: Clear processes and timelines for internal and external reporting to authorities.
Requirement: Procedures for performing Root Cause Analysis after incident resolution.
Pillar 3: Digital Operational Resilience Testing
Requirement: Establishment of a comprehensive testing program (e.g., vulnerability scans, penetration tests).
Requirement: Management and implementation of Threat-Led Penetration Testing (TLPT) for critical functions.
Requirement: Documentation and swift remediation of identified weaknesses.
Pillar 4: Managing ICT Third-Party Risk
Requirement: A register of all contractual arrangements concerning critical/important functions (CIFs).
Requirement: Requirement for documented exit strategies for critical ICT service providers.
Requirement: Contractual inclusion of full audit and inspection rights.
Pillar 5: Information Sharing
Requirement: Defined mechanisms for sharing cyber threat information and intelligence (TTPs, IoCs).
Requirement: Measures to protect the confidentiality and security of shared information.
